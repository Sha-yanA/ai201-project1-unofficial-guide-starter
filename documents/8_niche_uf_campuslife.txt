51% of students highly rate the dining facilities. 603 responses


Dining hall food at the University of Florida receives mixed reviews; some students find the food options to be decent with good variety including healthy and vegan choices, while others feel the quality and consistency could be improved. Common complaints relate to occasional spoiled items, undercooked food, and limited late-night options when dining halls are closed. Nonetheless, students appreciate the flexibility of meal plans that allow use of flex bucks at select restaurants off-campus.
